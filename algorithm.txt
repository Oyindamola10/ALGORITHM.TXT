Problem: Algorithm to read a sentence to determine:
1) Words count
2)Vowel count

Solution
-create variable to hold
   sentence, wordCount, vowelChar, vowelCount
-Read the sentence
-On getting to a space add 1 to wordCount
-continue counting until getting to point
-Read vowelChar(a,e,i,o,u)
-Read sentence char by char
-If sentence char match any vowelChar
-Add 1 to vowelCount
